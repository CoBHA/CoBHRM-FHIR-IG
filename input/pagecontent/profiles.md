The following profiles are used in this Implimentation Guide to represent the data that CoBHRM needs. Some of the profiles are defined in this IG, and some are from us-core. The profiles defined in this IG are based on the us-core profiles, and use the us-core profiles as their parent.  This allows for maximum reuse of the us-core profiles, while still allowing for the necessary customizations to meet the needs of CoBHRM.
